Classic Fluffy Pancakes

By Abubakar Sadiq Sambo

These classic fluffy pancakes are the perfect start to any day. They are incredibly easy to make with simple ingredients and can be customized with your favorite toppings. This recipe guarantees light and tender pancakes every time!

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Cooking Time: 15 minutes
Number of Servings: 4 servings (12 pancakes)
Difficulty Level: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the liquid mixture into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. The batter should be slightly lumpy. Do not overmix!
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour about ¼ cup of batter for each pancake onto the griddle. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, about 2-3 minutes. Flip the pancake and cook until golden brown on the other side.
  6. Serve immediately with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.

Tips and Notes

For extra fluffy pancakes, let the batter rest for 5-10 minutes before cooking. This allows the baking powder to activate properly. You can also add a teaspoon of vanilla extract to the wet ingredients for extra flavor.
